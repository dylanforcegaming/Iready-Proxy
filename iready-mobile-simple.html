💡 Getting "Desktop Only" Error?

i-Ready sometimes blocks mobile browsers. Here's how to fix it:

  1. Chrome: Tap ⋮ menu → Check "Desktop site"
  2. Safari: Tap AA button → "Request Desktop Website"
  3. Samsung: Tap ☰ → "Desktop site"
